About

TPM (Total People Management) is a 100% black female owned company with a commitment to actively participate in the skills development landscape through addressing and fulfilling the National Skills Development Strategy (NSDS lll) mandate of the South African Government. With a collective experience of over 30 years in people development and business management, we have in-depth knowledge, experience and leadership to offer our business partners tailor made and relevant business solutions with practical implementation.

  • Our Vision

    To be the “go to” and trusted service provider in the markets we serve.

  • Our Purpose

    We empower individuals and businesses to implement strategies that will transform their lives.

  • Our Values

    • Service Excellence
    • Transparency & integrity
    • Action
    • Respect
    • Social and Environmental Responsibility

  • Our Strategic Objectives

    • To bring business strategy to life by getting the basics right, thereby offering practical solutions.
    • To offer our clients management solutions of high quality, cost effective and reliable.
    • To learn and understand the character and culture of our business partners, so we can establish firm and long relationships that are mutually beneficial.
    • To empower our partners with learning solutions and implement strategies that will transform their lives.
    • To offer employees an opportunity to work in a dynamic, challenging and growth oriented environment.

Accreditations

We are accredited with various SETAs and collaborate with other reputable accredited providers. This means with all our training programmes, delegates will receive a valid certificate of competence while studying towards a nationally recognized qualification at their own pace, aligned to SAQA standards. This empowers and equips our delegates for better growth prospects in their chosen careers.
